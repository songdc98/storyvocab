# Code Of Conduct

StoryVocab welcomes constructive participation from learners, teachers, developers, and researchers.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and specific.
- Focus criticism on ideas, code, documentation, and user experience.
- Assume contributors may have different language backgrounds and technical levels.
- Keep discussion relevant to StoryVocab, language learning, accessibility, privacy, and deployment.
- Credit others' work when building on it.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or exclusionary behavior.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Spamming issues, pull requests, or contact channels.
- Pressuring maintainers to disclose private accounts, credentials, API keys, or personal data.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove comments, close issues, block participants, or restrict contribution access when behavior harms the project or community.
